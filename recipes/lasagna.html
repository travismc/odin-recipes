Lasagna

Delicious homemade lasagna

Make the best homemade lasagna you will ever taste.

Ingredients:

  • lasagna pasta
  • fresh tomatoes
  • parmesan cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • ricotta cheese
  • onion
  • garlic

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
