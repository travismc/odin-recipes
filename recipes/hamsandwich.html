Ham Sandwich

The best ham sandwich on the playground

Make the best ham sandwich you will ever taste.

Ingredients:

  • white bread
  • sliced ham
  • cheddar cheese
  • mustard
  • mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Spread mustard and mayonnaise on two slices of bread.
  2. Add sliced ham.
  3. Add sliced cheddar cheese.
  4. Cut into triangles and enjoy.
